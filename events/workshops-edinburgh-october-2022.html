--- layout: news-events filename: events title: October 2022 OMERO workshop main-blurb: a short 1h workshop given in-person at the University of Edinburgh. ---

Organisers: Advanced imaging resource at Institute of Genetics and Cancer

Location: Edinburgh, UK

Workshop: What can OMERO do for you and your Lab

Times and Dates: 12 October 2022, at 14.00

Duration: 1 hour

The overview of OMERO software was given with emphasis on the usage of OMERO by Group Leaders in an institution. The short session concentrated on giving pointers as to where find help about OMERO and usage of OMERO for an Lab in an Institution as well as publishing data with OMERO.
