Organisers: Global BioImaging

Location: held remotely

Seminar 1: Principles of FAIR data

Date: 24 February 2023

Seminar 2: Reuse of image data in IDR repository: Case studies

Date: 24 February 2023

Duration: 30 minutes for each of the two Seminars

The first seminar will introduce the FAIR principles as applied in case of Image Data Resource (IDR). The second seminar will then go over case studies and example of data reanalysis from IDR and usage of OME-NGFF. For more details and to apply for the workshop(s), please visit the site of the event.
