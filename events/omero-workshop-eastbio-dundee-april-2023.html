The OMERO demo/workshop is a part of a whole-day course for Postdoctoral Students.

Organiser: EastBio

Location: Dundee, UK

Date: 24th April 2023

Duration: 1.5 hour

This course focuses on explanation of basics of OMERO and OMERO.figure. Both parts give encouragement for hands-on work with concrete examples and tasks. The main theme of the workshop was the publication possibilities in OMERO and OMERO.figure.

The workshop concentrates on basics of OMERO and did not assume any programming skills.
