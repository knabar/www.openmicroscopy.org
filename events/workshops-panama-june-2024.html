Organisers: Smithsonian Tropical Research Institute

Location: Smithsonian Tropical Research Institute, Panama

Workshop: OMERO Workshop

Dates: 13 - 14 June 2024

OMERO, a component of OME, is a software platform for image data management and analysis which were presented in this workshop. Import, organisation, viewing, searching, annotation and publishing of imaging data with OMERO were shown.

Further, the emphasis was given on the popular OMERO.figure application.

During the second day, we concentrated on more advanced OMERO topics, showing connections of OMERO with popular 3rd party image analysis software packages such as Fiji, Cellpose or StarDist and using OMERO Python API for running image analysis on the cloud.
